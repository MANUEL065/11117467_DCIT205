Lets Begin

The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana offers a diverse range of short courses catering to various aspects of information technology. The "Microsoft SQL Server Database Administration" course (₵ 995) equips participants with skills in managing and maintaining Microsoft SQL Server databases, a fundamental aspect of modern database systems. The "Linux Administration" course (₵ 995) provides hands-on training in Linux server administration, crucial for those seeking proficiency in open-source operating systems. For those interested in cybersecurity, the "Information Security and Ethical Hacking" course (₵ 995) delves into the principles of securing information systems and ethical hacking techniques. The "Introduction to Web Programming" course (₵ 900) is designed to familiarize participants with the basics of web development, while the "Digital Marketing" course (₵ 900) focuses on strategies and tools for effective online marketing. The "Certificate in Software Development" course (₵ 1500) offers comprehensive training in software development, covering programming languages and development methodologies Additionally, the department provides practical courses such as "Phone and Laptop Repairs" (₵ 1000) for hands-on troubleshooting and repair skills. Courses like "Oracle Database Administration" (₵ 995) and "Data Science" (₵ 1500) explore Oracle database management and the principles of data science, respectively. The "Computer Networking Fundamentals - CCNA" course (₵ 995) introduces participants to the essentials of computer networking with a focus on Cisco's CCNA certification. "Introduction to Microsoft Office Suite" (₵ 750) caters to foundational skills in using Microsoft Office applications. Furthermore, the department offers courses in specialized areas like "Mobile Application Development" (₵ 1000) and "Certificate in Information Systems Audit" (₵ 995). These short courses collectively contribute to the holistic development of skills in the ever-evolving field of computer science.
