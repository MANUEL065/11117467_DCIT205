Our Ph.D. Programmes

The PhD Programs of the Computer Science Department of the University of Ghana aim to provide advanced training in research and development in computer science and computer technology. The programs are designed for interested students who wish to undertake advanced-level research work in computer science. The Computer Science Department of the University of Ghana require students to take courses, conduct research, and submit a dissertation. The courses aim to provide students with in-depth knowledge of current trends and developments in computer science and its related fields. The research component of the programs is a vital part of the PhD programs. It provides students with opportunities to contribute to the advancement of knowledge in computer science and its related fields, and to make innovative and significant contributions to the field. Students are provided with a supportive and stimulating research environment where they can work with world-class researchers and academics.

Course Description

CSCD 721Grand Research Challenges in Computer Science Wireless communication has become a ubiquitous part of modern life, from global cellular telephone systems to local and even personal-area networks. This course provides an in-depth introduction to digital mobile wireless networks, illustrating theoretical underpinnings with a wide range of real-world examples. The course will review propagation phenomena, modulation techniques, multiple access schemes, and coding techniques, air interface design, wireless network planning and operations, GSM, TDMA and CDMA technologies. The coursework is designed to provide a strong theoretical foundation and specialized knowledge in specific areas of computer science. Students are expected to engage in independent research, contribute to the academic field, and demonstrate a deep understanding of their chosen area of study.
