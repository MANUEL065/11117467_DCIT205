The Internal Competitions at the University of Ghana's Computer Science Department are an annual highlight, gathering students to showcase their coding prowess and problem-solving skills. Teams engage in friendly yet intense contests, solving real-world problems and presenting innovative projects. The event promotes collaboration, camaraderie, and practical application of theoretical knowledge. Judges, including faculty and industry professionals, evaluate projects and recognize outstanding achievements. Overall, the Internal Competitions contribute significantly to the academic and professional development of students, creating a dynamic and enriching experience within the department.

The External Competitions at the University of Ghana's Computer Science Department bring together students from various universities for a collaborative showcase of coding and problem-solving skills. This inter-university event fosters networking and idea exchange, emphasizing both individual and team excellence. Judges, including experts from academia and industry, assess projects, providing valuable insights. The competition enhances the department's reputation and exposes students to diverse perspectives, enriching their academic experience.
